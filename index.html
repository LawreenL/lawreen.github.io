About Me

I am a Senior at the University at Buffalo, studying Computer Science and Math (minor). I enjoy teaching, design, gardening, hackathons and music of all genres. I am interested in Human-Computer Interaction, Technology for Social Good and EdTech.

I love creative projects and solving problems. The only thing I enjoy more is helping others. I believe in exploring the possibilities of Computer Science and pushing it's boundaries for social good.
