IN-BETWEEN BOOKS

Posted by Lawreen Latif on November 30, 2016

Listen. Come here. You’ll want to read this.

I had never talked to this man before but hearing his words made me want to take a look. Leaning over the table, I peeked at the book he was holding towards me. It was the next book to the series I read two weeks ago. I had asked the librarian but someone else had already borrowed the book.

My eyes obviously betrayed every attempt I had made to contain my excitement. His chuckle gave away that he’d noticed.

Do you like John Green?

Well, not particularly. But somehow, I’ve managed to read quite a few of his books.

His answer didn’t surprise me. Suddenly, my hair seemed 10 times more silky. I tried to tuck it behind my ear but it slipped out again.

The next day, he handed me another book.

Found this at the bookstore. I think you’ll like it.

Why do you think I’ll like it?

Well, you’re always reading those romantic novels with the tiniest smile on your face?

I felt my cheeks heat up.

Somehow, noticing your reactions made me read the books you’ve read. And suddenly, I had read them all. He laughed.

I wondered how he was able to tell such embarrassing things without any restraint. Or did he not realize what he was saying?

Oh!

His eyes widened ever so slightly. I think he just realized. I couldn’t help but let out a giggle.

So have you been stalking me?

Only your books. He said with a playful smile.

Shh!

The cliched librarian shushed us. My head was already playing out all the romantic movies I had seen with this particular scene.

Want to spend a day with me? I know a great bookstore around Main Street.

I already knew which one, but the love guru in my head had already given its response.

Sure! Does this Sunday sound good?
