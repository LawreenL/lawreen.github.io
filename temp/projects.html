DAM

May 2019

Final project of our Software Engineering course. I worked with a team of 4 other students and the clients (students and professors) to build a resource management tool for our hardware library. We noticed the library had tools that students did not know about or know how to request them and decided to build a web app to help facilitate this. Users would be able to view the inventory and reserve them for set periods of time. Users were also seperated into general users, managers and admins. Used Django to build the webapp. The main areas I worked on included the clients loans and reservations functionality as well as managing permissions between user types. I also created the style guide and conducted A/B Testing. Our project was selected to use as a base for a live site for students to use. I also later went on to TA for this course!
